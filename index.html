About Me

I am currently a third-year student at the Yingcai Honors College of the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). My major is Mathematics-Physics Fundamental Science, which involves the selection of 24 students from approximately 5000 peers in the same academic year. My research focuses on deep learning and computer vision, with particular experiences in research related to generative models.

In 2022, I joined the Center for Future Media in UESTC, where I engage in learning and research under the guidance of Professor Lianli Gao. Currently, I have completed two research projects as a co-first author and a second author respectively. These works are submitted to ECCV and TCSVT The research "Make-A-Storyboard" focuses on addressing the issue of incoherence in generating images in existing story visualization. It proposes a universal framework for the Image Customization Method, which is readily applicable as a plug-and-play solution. On the other hand, "Motionzero" concentrates on enhancing the zero-shot video generation by leveraging motion priors obtained from LLMs.

Since March 2024, I have been working as a research assistant in the StatsLE Lab at the Department of Statistical Sciences, University of Toronto, under the supervision of Professor Qiang Sun. My research focuses on statistics and generative artificial intelligence, particularly on the theory and methods of diffusion models. I have co-authored one paper during this period, titled “B Vitamin Dynamics During Pregnancy and the Risk of Postpartum Anemia,” in which I am the third author. I was responsible for the statistical analysis of the data and the construction of the postpartum anemia prediction model. This paper is currently under review at Nature Communications.
